Atrial Fibrillaiton Project

"Any department who is taking care or seeing atrial fibrillation patient can participate in our study. The study is not restricted to cardiology or internal medicine department."

Project Co-ordinators
For one state, We can have a maximum of three projects co-ordinators. Below are duties of Project Co-ordinators.
  • Administering the project in the respective state it includes co-ordinating the collaborators regarding the steps of project.
  • Adressing the queries of different colloaborators in the respective state.
  • Monitering the study quality and helping collaborators in data collection.
Project Collaborators
There are no restriction for the number of collaboratos. However from one department or unit of hospital we can have maximum two collaborators.
  • Carrying out the study in the respective department of the hospital.
  • Getting the IRB approval (ethic clearence) from the institution ethics committee.
  • Collecting data at the local site.
  • Sending the final data to the project team.

How to involve in study.
Follow the steps below to participate in the project.
  • Register yourself for the project.
  • Download the protocol and submit it to the IRB or ethics department of your college or hospital for the carrying our the study.
  • Once you get the ethics approval send the copy of ethics approval to the project co-ordinator.
  • Our project co-ordinator will verify the IRB or ethics approval and after that they will send you the link for collecting the data.
  • Failure to provide copy of IRB or ethics clearence will cause removal from study and no authorship will be given in such case.
  • Collect the data at the time specified. After the data collection send it to the project co-ordinator and fill the authorship form.
